Habuild — Home Screen Revamp

Three directions, built from your real current home (World Record Week + Strong Everyday sessions, the promo carousel, Box Breathing, Daily Habits, Quick Actions). Applying Saurabh's feedback: smaller session cards, no moving carousel, an upcoming section that reads like a calendar, and far less vertical scroll. Colors and type from the Habuild "Warm Sky" design system. Tuned for 45+: 15px+ text, high contrast, big tap targets, zero auto-motion.

The 4 problems in the current screen → the fix

  • Session cards too big. Two full-width cards with instructor photos. Fix: slim time-rows, ~70% less height, photos gone for returning members.
  • Repetitive copy. "6:30 AM · 45 min · World Record Week · Lower Body" twice over. Fix: time + name + one action only.
  • Moving carousel = lost 45+ member. 3 auto-rotating promos. Fix: one static World Record strip; promos don't move.
  • Three tile-grids deep. Breathe + Daily Habits + Quick Actions stacked. Fix: merge Box Breathing into Daily Habits → one grid, screen ends sooner.
A — Today's Agenda Recommended
Sessions become one chronological list — done (✓), live now, what's next. The cleanest answer to "no carousel, no repetition." Whole home fits in ~1.5 screens instead of 3.
